Jim Rohn

American Entrepreneur and Speaker

Jim's Life

  • Born September 17, 1930
  • Age 19 - Quit college because he knew enough - a major mistake
  • Age 26 - Broke despite being a hard worker, he met Earl Shoaf and turned his life around due to a pursuit of personal philosophy
  • Age 33 - Began public speaking after becoming a millionaire in only 5 years' time
  • Throughout his life he continued to speak and inspire
  • Died December 5, 2009
Info provided from Wikipedia and Jim's Audiobook - The Art of Exceptional Living
